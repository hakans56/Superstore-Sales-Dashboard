# 🛒 Superstore Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

## 📊 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive sales performance dashboard** built using **Power BI** and the popular **Superstore dataset**.
The goal of this analysis is to explore sales trends, profitability, and regional performance while providing a clear and interactive business intelligence dashboard.

The dashboard helps answer key business questions such as:

* How are **sales and profits performing over time?**
* Which **cities and states generate the most revenue?**
* What are the **top-performing products by profit?**
* How do **different regions and categories contribute to overall sales?**
* What is the **Year-over-Year (YoY) growth in sales?**

---

# 🔑 Key Metrics

The dashboard highlights several important business KPIs:

* **Total Sales:** $2.30M
* **Total Profit:** $286.4K
* **Total Orders:** 5K
* **Sales Last Year:** $1.6M
* **Year-over-Year Growth:** $733.2K
* **YoY Growth %:** 46.9%

These KPIs provide a quick overview of the company's performance.

---

# 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Sales & Profit Trend Analysis

A time-series visualization shows how **sales and profit evolve month by month**, helping identify seasonality and performance patterns.

### 2️⃣ Top Performing Locations

The dashboard highlights:

* **Top 10 Cities by Sales**
* **Top 10 States by Sales**

This allows stakeholders to quickly identify **high-performing markets**.

### 3️⃣ Regional Sales Distribution

A bar chart compares **total sales across regions**, revealing geographical sales performance differences.

### 4️⃣ Product Profitability

The **Top 5 Products by Profit** visual identifies which products generate the highest profitability.

### 5️⃣ Category Performance

A category-level comparison shows how **Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies** contribute to total sales.
